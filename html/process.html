The Process

Initial Consultation

I like to meet with all of my potential clients for 30 minutes just to get to know them! I want to make sure we can build a relationship that we can both trust and that we are both the right fit for each other!

Trial Catalog

I will have you create a step-by-step process you use for each image and then send me 30 unedited pictures with 6 anchor images from a catalog that you have already edited. Then the trial catalog will be sent to me and then returned with my edits

Onboarding

If you choose to continue working with me, then I will send you the onboarding steps!
