About me

My family!

I am married to the most supportive man, Grant Christensen, and we have a one year old boy named Donovan. We enjoy watching the Utah Jazz, playing fetch with our dog Zoe, and cuddling up on the couch. Our favorite Netflix series is Schitt's Creek but we miss The Office.

Why editing?

My reasoning to be in this profession is most likely the opposite reason you’re here. I need less time to myself. As a stay at home mother, I found myself searching for more in life. I knew I needed a challenge, other than how well I can cut food into small pieces or count to 10 in a silly voice. :) I fulfilled this void with editing and put all my time and energy in becoming the best editor I possibly can for my clients.
