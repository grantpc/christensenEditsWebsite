Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

I can return an edited catalog back in 3-5 business days. If you need anything expedited, we can talk about the process for this! After all, my main goal is to help you so lets chat about it!

What is the process to send and receive images?

I use google drive to send and receive smart previews through Lightroom Classic. We will go over this is detail on the initial consultation call along with a PDF step-by-step form I will send to you. I want to make sure this is a very easy process for you to adapt!

Do you edit galleries other than weddings?

YES! I love all types of galleries! Weddings, family, engagement, business marketing, bridals, etc. You name it, I will edit it. 30 images minimum.

Do you offer culling services?

At this time, I do not offer culling services. However, if you are absolutely in a bind, let's talk!

What is the initial consultation?

After I receive your interest in learning more about the process, I will reach out to you through the email you gave me. We will set up a time to meet for 30 min, via zoom or in person, to just get to know each other and talk about next steps if you choose to do so. My goal in this consultation is to see if we are both a good fit for each other!

How do you learn my editing style?

After creating quick a step-by-step process for each image you edit, I will have you send me 30 images that you will edit yourself as well with 6 anchor images. Then I will compare my edits to your edits to learn as efficiently and effectively as possible. The sooner I can learn your style, the better for the both of us!
